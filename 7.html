Is SQL a good place for business logic?

by

posted on: November 11, 2015

&lt;TL;DR&gt; No, it isn&#8217;t. While SQL is a Turing-complete language and we can use it to encode any business logic we want, placing business (domain) logic into SQL leads to a less maintainable solution comparing to one that uses an OO or functional language. Because of that, I advocate to limit the use of SQL to read-only queries (which can potentially contain business logic, that&#8217;s fine) and simple CRUD statements where possible.
