Cool thing in Vs.Net 2003

by Oren Eini

posted on: March 05, 2005

Press Ctrl+Alt+Shift inside a defination (method, variable, property, etc) and press the up or down error. The entire defination (in my case it was a method), will move up or down, not just one line, it'll jump over other definations! It's going to be very useful in arranging related methods together, I discovered it by accident, and I've no idea whatever it's a VS thing or Resharper thing. Cool nonetheless, though.

The Sims 2: The Architect View

by Oren Eini

posted on: March 04, 2005

My father work in construction, and he had got my younger brother (who plays Sims 2 quite a bit) to build him detailed plans of his project, and then he can just walk in and see all the parts connecting. Quite a bit more useful and easy to use than most CAD software, and produce results that you consume immediately (or show clients :-)). Quite a way to sell a house, you get both VR and ease of editing. Think that this wall will look better 30 Centimeters to the left, just move it and see the affect. WYSIWYG for architects. Too bad that you've to use cheats to get this affect.
